Add the bus schedule to your phone or computer. No App Store required on iPhone.

Open SRTA Bus Schedule

iPhone / iPad: Add to Home Screen

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. Tap Share (square with arrow up).
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. The bus icon appears on your Home Screen.

After installing, open the app from your Home Screen — not from a browser tab — for the full-screen app experience.

Android: Install App

Tap the button below, or use Chrome menu → Install app / Add to Home screen.

  1. Open in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu (⋮) → Install app or Add to Home screen.
